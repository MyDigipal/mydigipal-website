Q: Is it necessary to invest a large budget to be present on social media?
A: No, a huge budget is not always necessary. Social media platforms offer flexible options that allow you to adjust your spending according to your goals and audience. Moreover, well-targeted strategies, even with a modest budget, can yield significant results.

Q: Can you advertise on multiple Facebook pages simultaneously?
A: Yes, it is entirely possible to manage advertising campaigns on multiple Facebook pages at the same time. This can even be advantageous for segmenting your campaigns according to different target audiences or for promoting various offers on different pages.

Q: How important is targeting in social media campaigns?
A: Targeting is crucial for maximizing the effectiveness of your campaigns. Social media targeting tools allow you to reach qualified prospects based on specific criteria such as location, age, and online behavior, thereby reducing your conversion costs.

Q: How can we ensure the effectiveness of paid social campaigns?
A: We design ads with catchy headlines and compelling descriptions, while optimizing to attract and engage potential customers.

Q: What support do you offer for paid social campaigns?
A: We provide expert advice, assist with content creation, continuously optimize campaigns, and offer technical support throughout the process.

Q: Is it possible to advertise on less traditional platforms like TikTok or Spotify for automotive purposes?
