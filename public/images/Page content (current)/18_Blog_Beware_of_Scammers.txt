BEWARE OF SCAMMERS - PEOPLE IMPERSONATING MYDIGIPAL

Scammers are impersonating MyDigipal with fake job offers! They contact victims via WhatsApp & direct them to fraudulent sites. Stay alert & verify!

It has come to our attention that a group of scammers is impersonating MyDigipal to deceive individuals by offering fake job opportunities. Their scheme is highly elaborate, and they operate as follows:
