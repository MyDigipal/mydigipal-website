## DMD Group Success Story

DMD Group, a cornerstone of the automotive industry in France's Grand Ouest region since 1934, faced the challenge of modernizing their digital advertising. With over 45 dealerships and 1,000+ vehicles in stock, they needed a scalable solution.

### The challenge

Competing in a crowded market, DMD sought to:
- Automate advertising across 45+ locations
- Reach qualified buyers with personalized ads
- Reduce cost per lead while increasing volume
- Maintain consistent brand presence across platforms

### The solution

Using Dynamic Ads across Facebook, Instagram, and Google, DMD automated their entire advertising operation:

- **Unified catalog** syncing all 1,000+ vehicles daily
- **Personalized campaigns** targeting buyers by vehicle preference
- **Promotional overlays** for "Offer of the Week" and "Offer of the Month"
- **Cross-platform reach** maximizing visibility on Meta and Google

### The results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Cost per Lead | €28 | €3.50 |
| Monthly Leads | 45 | 320 |
| Setup Time | 20+ hours/week | < 1 hour/week |
| Platform Coverage | Facebook only | Facebook, Instagram, Google |
