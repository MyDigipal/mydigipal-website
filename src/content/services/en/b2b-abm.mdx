## Our ABM process

Account-based marketing works best when strategy, execution, and measurement are tightly aligned. We follow a proven four-step process that turns high-value accounts into predictable revenue.

### 1. Identify - Build your target account list

We start by defining your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) and building a prioritized account list based on firmographic data, intent signals, and your CRM history. Every account is scored so your team focuses on the opportunities most likely to close.

### 2. Engage - Multi-channel orchestration

We launch coordinated campaigns across LinkedIn, programmatic display, email sequences, and content syndication. Each touchpoint is personalized to the account's industry, pain points, and buying stage - ensuring your message resonates with every stakeholder in the decision-making unit.

### 3. Activate - Sales and marketing alignment

ABM only works when sales and marketing operate as one team. We build shared dashboards, define handoff protocols, and equip your sales reps with account-specific insights and content so they can have more relevant conversations with prospects.

### 4. Measure - Pipeline attribution

We track every interaction from first touch to closed deal, connecting marketing spend directly to pipeline and revenue. You get clear visibility into which accounts are progressing, which channels drive engagement, and what your true cost per opportunity looks like.
