## Our paid social process

Every successful campaign starts with a clear strategy and ends with actionable data. Our four-step process ensures your ad spend consistently delivers measurable business outcomes.

### 1. Audit and strategy

We analyze your current social advertising performance, competitive landscape, and audience data. From there, we build a channel strategy with clear KPIs, budget allocation, and a creative roadmap tailored to your sales cycle.

### 2. Creative and targeting

Our team develops scroll-stopping ad creative - from static visuals to short-form video - while building precise audience segments using job titles, company lists, intent data, and lookalike modeling. Every ad speaks directly to your buyer's pain points.

### 3. Launch and optimize

We launch campaigns with structured A/B testing across audiences, creatives, and placements. Continuous optimization happens daily - adjusting bids, pausing underperformers, and scaling what works based on real pipeline data, not just vanity metrics.

### 4. Scale and report

Once we identify winning combinations, we scale budget toward the highest-performing campaigns while maintaining cost efficiency. Monthly reports connect ad performance directly to pipeline and revenue, giving you full transparency on ROI.

- **LinkedIn Ads** - The gold standard for B2B targeting. We leverage Sponsored Content, Message Ads, Lead Gen Forms, and Document Ads to reach decision-makers by title, seniority, company size, and industry.
