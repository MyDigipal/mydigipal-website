## Why email marketing in 2025?

While social media and new channels grab headlines, email remains the undisputed champion of digital marketing ROI. In 2025, email marketing is experiencing a powerful renaissance, driven by AI personalization and smart automation.

### The Numbers Don't Lie

- **$36-40 ROI** for every dollar spent - the highest of any marketing channel
- **4.6 billion** email users worldwide by 2025
- **41% of marketers** rank email as their most effective channel
- **40x more effective** at customer acquisition than social media

### But There's a Catch

Generic, one-size-fits-all emails don't work anymore. With inboxes overflowing, only personalized, relevant messages cut through the noise. That's where AI-powered personalization makes the difference.
